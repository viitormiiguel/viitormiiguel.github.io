Vitor Miguel Xavier Peres is student in the field of Computer Science with a focus on Computer Vision. He holds a Master's degree in Computer Science from the Pontifical Catholic University of Rio Grande do Sul (PUCRS). His research interests include Facial Expression Recognition and Computer Vision. He holds an MBA in Digital Marketing from ESPM (2016), and a Bachelors degree in Information Systems from Uniritter (2014).

I work in the area of Computer Vision, focusing on the Recognition of Spontaneous Facial Expressions. having as main objective, to analyze and validate a model that allows the identification of spontaneous faces in an uncontrolled environment.
